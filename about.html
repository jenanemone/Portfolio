About Me

I first discovered a love of code around the year 2000. The first major project I worked on was an assistive technology system to help blind and visually impaired people access visual data.

Then I took a detour into business with my husband. I saw a huge opportunity with his side hustle. Within six months of my diving in, we had moved out of the garage and into a proper warehouse. It took several more years to achieve full speed. We are now both pursuing other interests. What this means for you is that you'll have someone who truly understands what a business needs.

In 2020, like many others, I reevaluated my professional interests and realized that I missed coding. I'm much happier building things on the internet.
